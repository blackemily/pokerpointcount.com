Pressure Changes Everything

Many players perform well early and struggle late.

As stacks shrink, payouts grow, and opportunities become fewer, pressure begins influencing decisions.

Endgame Strategy teaches you how to stay disciplined when the tournament reaches its most important stages.

The later the game becomes, the more valuable good decisions become.

Why Players Collapse Late

The endgame exposes weaknesses.

  • Fear of busting
  • Fear of missing payouts
  • Desperation from short stacks
  • Overconfidence from big stacks
  • Emotional decision-making

These mistakes become expensive because there is less time available to recover from them.

The Checkpoint Poker Endgame Framework

Every decision becomes more important near the end.

The Checkpoint Poker system helps players focus on decision quality rather than emotion, fear, or short-term results.

You learn how to recognize opportunities, avoid unnecessary risks, and maintain discipline when pressure rises.

Strong endgame players understand that survival, pressure, and opportunity must remain in balance.

Who This Book Is For

This book is for players who consistently reach important stages but struggle to finish strong.

It is for players who want a structured approach to late-stage decision making.
